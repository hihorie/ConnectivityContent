Expedia Partner Hackathon

24-26 April 2017  |  Expedia Office, Madrid

Plaza de Carlos Trias Bertrán, 7, 28020 Madrid, Spain

 

Join us in Madrid for an exclusive opportunity to optimize your customers experience in the Global Expedia marketplace!

Let’s put our minds together and explore unique scenarios that can positively influence your business and the travel industry. Expedia engineers will be on site, sharing valuable information that will enable you to build creative solutions using Expedia’s industry-leading and exclusive experimental API’s.

This event is a unique opportunity to discuss fun travel & tech ideas and hack these concepts into reality for a chance to win cool prizes.

Judging Criteria

30% Creativity & Innovation
How new and innovative is the chosen use case

30% Implementation completeness
Working code trumps over fancy PowerPoint!

20% Business Potential

20% Innovative usage of Expedia APIs

Must utilize a supply-partner API to be considered for a prize.

Prizes per team

1st:   200,000 Expedia+ Points

2nd:  100,000 Expedia+ Points

3rd:   50,000 Expedia+ Points

 

Event Guidelines

  • Come with ideas not completed code. We want you to brainstorm within your company about how you would use our APIs in your solution, which means you may need to write some code to understand our APIs to take full advantage of working with our engineers. But don't go beyond tinkering with our APIs. This is meant to be a fun event and we want all teams to hack on site.
  • Team must demo a functional application to be eligible for prizes.
  • All applications will be judged against the criteria published here. Stay tuned for an update on criteria soon!
  • Teams will have access to Expedia engineering and API experts for guidance and help.
  • Teams will have access to Expedia mentors for guidance on areas like business use cases, Value add, Impact, Demo presentations.
  • Hack. Meet new people. Revolutionize Travel. Have fun while doing it!
